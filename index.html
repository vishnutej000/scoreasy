Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our mentorship program

🎓

What qualifications do your mentors have?

+

All our mentors are verified educators with advanced degrees from top universities. They undergo rigorous screening and have proven track records in academic mentoring with years of teaching experience.

⏰

How flexible are the session timings?

+

Sessions can be scheduled according to your availability. We offer morning, afternoon, and evening slots across different time zones to accommodate students worldwide.

📅

What is the typical program duration?

+

Program duration varies based on your goals. Most students see significant improvement within 3-6 months of consistent mentoring, with some achieving their targets even sooner.

💻

What technology do I need for sessions?

+

You only need a computer or tablet with internet connection. We use user-friendly video conferencing tools that work on any device, plus digital whiteboard and screen sharing capabilities.

💳

What payment options are available?

+

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and offer monthly or annual payment plans. All transactions are secure and encrypted with industry-standard security protocols.

📈

Do you guarantee results?

+

While we cannot guarantee specific grades, 95% of our students report improved academic performance within the first semester of mentoring. We're committed to your success!

🤖

How does the mentor matching work?

+

Our AI algorithm analyzes your academic profile, learning style, and goals to match you with the most compatible mentor from our network of expert educators.

📚

What subjects do you cover?

+

We cover all major subjects including Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), English, History, and specialized areas like SAT/ACT prep and university applications.
